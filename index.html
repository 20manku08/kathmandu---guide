Welcome to the Beating Heart of Nepal

Kathmandu sits inside a beautiful bowl-shaped valley in the Himalayas, positioned at an altitude of approxiametly 1,400 meters. It is a living, breathing museum where modern urban lifestyles interlock seamlessly with ancient traditions that have been preserved for centuries.

Whether you are seeking the spiritual energy of centuries-old stupas or looking to treat your tastebuds to incredibly rich spices, this hub is designed to walk you through the essential experiences of our capital city.

🏛

Architectural Heritage

The valley acts as a structural host to seven distinct zones of UNESCO World Heritage Sites, boasting complex wooden arts, high stone pillars, and historic royal palaces.
